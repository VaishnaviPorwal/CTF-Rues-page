RULES


Welcome.

CTF or Capture the Flags is a kind of cyber security competition in which "flags" are secretly hidden in purposefully-vulnerable programs or websites.

Eligibility:

  • The contest is open to all first year students only.
  • Participants should register themselves through the google form provided by Computer Engineering Society, MMMUT. If not do register yourself first there.

How to Enter:

Eligible participants may enter by registering at https://jctf.cesmmmut.ml/challenges and completing the tasks as detailed on the site. Participants may enter only once.

Right to Cancel:

If for any reason the Contest is not capable of running as planned, including tampering, unauthorized intervention, fraud, technical failures, printing errors, or any other causes which corrupt or affect the administration, security, fairness, integrity, or proper conduct of the Contest, Computer Engineering Society, MMMUT reserves the right at its sole discretion to cancel, terminate, modify or suspend the Contest. CES further reserves the right to disqualify any entrant who tampers with the submission process or contest, cheats, deceives, abuses, annoys, threatens any other entrants or Judges, or otherwise violates any law or behaves in an unfit manner as determined by CES.

It is strictly prohibited and subject to disqualification:

  • Attack any machine other than those designated
  • Fraudulently modify the dashboard
  • Intentionally prevent other teams from finding flags
  • Share answers on challenges with anyone before the end of the competition



Regards
Computer Engineering Society, MMMUT
